**{user} here are a list of commands to use**
***Moderation/Server related***
-`.commandhelp` (brings this menu up)
-`.serverupdates` (server custom command updates)
-`.report <@(admin name)> <@(user name)> <reason>` (reports a user to a admin of your choice) 
-`.mod` (Lists moderation commands)
-`.admin` (admin only commands)
-`.subscribe` (subscribe to recieve updates)
-`.extlist` (Server extension)
-`.enter` (Invite to Fanthom's server)
-`.request <@(mod name or admin name)> <what you want to request>` (Request anything to a certain mod or admin)
***Miscellaneous***
-`.clickwikisearch <what you want to search>` (Searches "Click wiki")
-`.clickwikiforcesearch <what you want to search>` (forces results from "Click wiki")
-`.search <what you want to search>` (Search google)
-`.shaders` (Experimental)
***Coming soon***
